OpDecision AI

Your personalized AI-Powered Decision Support.

The Problem

Robbie and Don's vision is to maintain a company's knowledge base when it comes to operations decision-making. Robbie's grandpa is a business owner and knows exactly how much to order of each product to keep in stock, when to run promotions, and how to price. But what happens when he retires? We want to capture that intuition and make it available to the next generation of operators, so that they can make informed decisions and keep the business running smoothly.

The Solution

OpDecision AI is an AI-powered decision support tool that captures and preserves the knowledge and intuition of business owners and operators. OpDecision AI analyzes historical data, identifies patterns, and provides actionable insights to help businesses make informed decisions. This ensures that valuable knowledge is retained and accessible to future generations of operators, ultimately improving decision-making and business performance.

New research by supply chain professor Leela Nageswaran at the Foster's Business School at the University of Washington has shown that qualitative AI could help students make better decisions in a simulated inventory management game - better by statistically significant amount than students who had access to quantitative AI or no AI. Our OpDecision AI is a qualitative AI, which means it'll assist operators by providing reccomendations based off mathematical models in natural language, enhancing understanding of decisions for operators, but not making decisions for them. This allows operators to utilize their own experiences in placing orders, but with guidance from the company's founder through a chatbox medium.
